About Us
As one of the largest manufacturers and distributors in Nigeria, we deliver trusted laundry bar soap, multipurpose soap, and personal hygiene products designed for everyday excellence.

Intecil Products Ltd was formed as a result of the entrepreneurial drive to join Nigeria’s growing market space for household products. The company’s aim has always been to provide a range of products that are sustainable and eco-friendly. INTECIL Products Ltd is now a household name in the soap & detergent industry in Nigeria. Our product range includes laundry bar soaps, multipurpose soaps, petroleum jelly, hand wash, baby care products, and beauty soaps. We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. With a strong distribution network across Nigeria, we ensure that our products are accessible to consumers nationwide.
