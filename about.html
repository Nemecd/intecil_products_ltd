About Us

Company Overview

Intecil Products Ltd, a subsidiary of the Intecil Group of Companies, was formed as a result of an entrepreneurial drive to participate in Nigeria’s rapidly growing household products market. The company’s aim has always been to provide a wide range of sustainable and eco-friendly products that meet everyday consumer needs.

Today, INTECIL Products Ltd has become a trusted household name in Nigeria’s soap and detergent industry. Our product portfolio includes laundry bar soaps, multipurpose soaps, petroleum jelly, hand wash, baby care products, and beauty soaps. We pride ourselves on our unwavering commitment to quality, continuous innovation, and customer satisfaction. Backed by a strong nationwide distribution network, we ensure our products are readily available to consumers across Nigeria.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, sustainable, and eco-friendly household products that enhance the daily lives of our customers while promoting environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative household products in Nigeria, recognized for our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and environmental stewardship.
